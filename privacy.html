Privacy Policy

Prosper Business Analytics Pty Ltd (trading as Outcomes Tracking)

This Privacy Policy describes how Prosper Business Analytics Pty Ltd (trading as Outcomes Tracking, "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when registering for and using the Outcomes Tracking platform, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, organisation name)
  • Performance data entered by users through the platform and mobile application (activity logs, step data, conversion records)
  • Usage data (how you interact with the platform)

Outcomes Tracking does not collect GPS or location data, record audio or video, or monitor device activity outside of the application.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Outcomes Tracking platform
  • Display performance analytics and reporting features
  • Provide onboarding and customer support
  • Improve the platform and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. Data may be shared with trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g. cloud hosting), under confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, contact us at kara@outcomestracking.com.
