Outcomes Tracking for F2F Fundraising Teams

Purpose-built for charity acquisition teams in Australia and New Zealand. PFRA-aware. Trust-based. Live in a day.

The fundraising performance problem

Results without context

You know how many donors your team signed yesterday. You don't know whether the shortfall was an approach rate problem, a pitch problem, or a close problem — and those need completely different fixes.

Generic coaching

Without step-level data, every coaching session covers the same ground. Time is wasted on parts of the process that are working fine while the actual gap goes unaddressed.

Inconsistent campaign performance

Some days your team fires. Other days, results fall flat. Without Activity and Skill data, you can't see the pattern — or build systematically on what works.

Visibility at every step of the fundraising conversation

Define your sales process steps to match your approach — Introduction, Pitch, Needs Analysis, DDM Close, Rehash, whatever terminology your campaign uses. Outcomes Tracking measures conversion at every step, for every rep, in real time.

Directors see team-level patterns. Team Leaders know exactly who to coach and on what. Reps see their own numbers and self-correct.

  • Approach-to-conversation rate (Activity tracking)
  • Step-by-step conversion through the pitch process
  • Individual rep Skill Ratio vs. team benchmark
  • Daily, shift, and campaign-level analytics
  • Mobile app for real-time logging in the field

F2F Fundraising FAQ

Is Outcomes Tracking suitable for PFRA-regulated campaigns?
Yes. Outcomes Tracking was built with the AU/NZ face-to-face fundraising market in mind. It has no GPS tracking, no call recording, and no consent requirements beyond standard campaign practice. The trust-based, rep-logged model is designed specifically for regulated F2F environments.
Can we customise the steps to match our pitch structure?
Yes. You define up to 6 steps in your sales process using your own terminology. Introduction, Pitch, DDM, Rehash — whatever language your campaign uses. The platform tracks conversion through your specific process, not a generic template.
How do fundraisers log data in the field?
Through the mobile app (iOS and Android). Reps log each interaction as it happens — tap through the steps reached, mark the outcome, and move on. It takes seconds. Data appears in dashboards immediately.
Does it work for both street and door-to-door fundraising?
Yes. The platform works for any face-to-face acquisition format — street, door-to-door, shopping centres, transport hubs, or events. The activity logging model adapts to how your team works.
