Door-to-Door Sales

Outcomes Tracking for Door-to-Door Sales Teams

Know whether results are an Activity problem or a Skill problem — and coach accordingly. Real-time visibility into every rep, every step, every shift.

The door-to-door performance gap

Volume vs. conversion confusion

Is a rep underperforming because they're not knocking enough doors — or because their conversion at the door opener is low? The answer changes the coaching completely, but most managers can't tell the difference.

Step blindness

A rep can have a good intro and a terrible close. Or they pitch well but can't rehash. Without step-level data, all of this is invisible — and coaching is wasted on the wrong things.

No live feedback loop

In door-to-door sales, teams are spread across suburbs. Managers can't be everywhere. Without real-time data, a bad shift is over before anyone can course-correct.

Real-time visibility across a distributed team

Reps log their activity through the mobile app as they work — doors knocked, conversations started, and progress through each step of the sales process. Managers see everything in real time, from anywhere.

Not just results — the process that produces them. Every rep's conversion at every step, updated as the shift unfolds.

  • Interactions and activity volume per rep per shift
  • Step-by-step conversion through your sales process
  • Team Leader view across all reps simultaneously
  • Day-by-day and campaign-level trend analysis
  • Benchmark tracking vs. company standards

No GPS. Just clean performance data.

Outcomes Tracking doesn't need to know where your reps are. It tracks what matters — Activity and Skill. No location tracking. No recordings. No consent risk.

Reps log their own numbers. This builds ownership and accountability, and typically produces more accurate data than passive tracking systems.

  • No GPS — location data never collected
  • No call recording — no consent complications
  • Rep-logged data creates ownership, not resentment
  • Simpler than CRM-dependent competitors

Door-to-door sales FAQ

How do reps log data while they're working?
Through the Outcomes Tracking mobile app on their phone. After each interaction, they log the outcome — which step they reached, what happened, and the result. It takes seconds. Data syncs to dashboards immediately.
Can we track different products or campaigns separately?
Yes. The platform supports multiple teams and configurations. You can set different process steps and benchmarks for different campaigns or product lines.
Do we need a CRM to use Outcomes Tracking?
No. Outcomes Tracking is a fully standalone platform. No CRM integration required. Most teams are live within a day.
Is the pricing per rep?
No. Outcomes Tracking is priced at US$60/week flat for your whole company. Unlimited reps, unlimited Team Leaders, unlimited managers. No per-seat fees.
