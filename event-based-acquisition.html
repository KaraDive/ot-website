Event-Based Acquisition

Outcomes Tracking for Event-Based Acquisition Teams

Shopping centres. Festivals. Supermarkets. Pop-up sites. Track rep performance at every event with the same precision as a permanent campaign.

The event acquisition challenge

Every event is different

Foot traffic varies. Venue types vary. Even weather affects conversion. Without consistent data collection, you can't separate the environmental factors from the performance factors.

Team deployment guesswork

Which venue performs best? Which rep thrives in shopping centres versus festivals? Without event-level data, these decisions are made on gut feeling and past impressions.

⏱

Short shifts, hard to optimise

Event-based work often involves 4–6 hour shifts with rapid team rotation. Feedback needs to be fast. Without real-time data, the window to course-correct closes before anyone sees the numbers.

Per-rep, per-shift, per-event visibility

Outcomes Tracking captures Activity and Skill at the shift level. Compare rep performance across different venues. Identify which event formats produce the best Skill Ratios. See which reps thrive in high-traffic environments versus quieter settings.

Real-time data means Team Leaders can make decisions in the moment — not after the event is over and it's too late to change anything.

  • Shift-level activity and conversion data per rep
  • Event-type and venue performance comparison
  • Real-time Team Leader view from any device
  • Daily averages and campaign-level rollups
  • Step-by-step breakdown through your sales process

What changes when you can see every shift

Smarter deployment

Know which venues and event types produce the best results for which reps. Deploy strategically, not by rotation.

In-shift coaching

If a rep's conversion is low at step 2 by midday, the Team Leader can intervene before the shift ends — not at the debrief.

Campaign benchmarking

Set Activity and Skill benchmarks per event type. Know immediately when performance drifts from standard.

Rep development

See individual rep progress across events over time. Track training impact with before-and-after data.

Event acquisition FAQ

Can we track different event types separately?
Yes. You can configure the platform for different campaign types and venues. Team Leaders can filter and compare data by event to identify which formats produce the best results for specific reps.
How do reps log data at a busy event site?
Through the mobile app. It takes seconds per interaction — reps tap through the steps they reached after each conversation. The log is simple enough to maintain even in high-traffic environments.
Does it work for seasonal or short-run campaigns?
Yes. There's no lock-in contract. Activate for a campaign, pause when it's over. The flat-rate pricing means cost doesn't scale with team size during peak periods.
