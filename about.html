About Outcomes Tracking

Built from inside the face-to-face world. Designed to solve the problems we lived with every day.

Built by people who've stood on doorsteps

Outcomes Tracking wasn't designed in a corporate boardroom by people who'd never worked a door-to-door shift. It came from the field. From years of managing face-to-face fundraising teams, watching talented reps underperform because nobody — including them — could see where the problem actually was.

The technology existed to track results. What didn't exist was a tool built for the specific rhythm of F2F work — fast-moving, rep-led, with a repeatable process that could be measured step by step.

So we built it.

The problem we set out to solve

In face-to-face sales, managers see results. They don't see process. They know who hit their numbers and who didn't — but they can't see why. Was it an Activity problem (not seeing enough people)? Or a Skill problem (not converting well enough at a particular step)? Without that distinction, coaching is generic and growth is accidental.

Existing tools either tracked outcomes only, required expensive CRM integrations, charged per seat (pricing out small and medium organisations), or were built for industries where GPS tracking and call recording were acceptable — not the trust-based, relationship-driven world of F2F fundraising and door-to-door sales.

The gap was clear: a platform built specifically for the Activity-and-Skill model of face-to-face performance, priced for whole-team use, deployable in a day, and built on a trust-based data model where reps log their own numbers.
