Why flat-rate pricing matters

Scale without penalty

Per-seat pricing punishes you for growing. Every new rep added costs more. Our model prices by Overview Users only — your field rep headcount never affects your bill.

Whole-team visibility

Pricing is tied to Overview Users — your Team Leaders — not your total headcount. Field reps get full mobile app access at every tier, regardless of how many you have.

No lock-in

No minimum contract term. No cancellation fees. The platform earns its place every week — and we're confident it will.

Immediate ROI

The platform pays for itself the first time targeted coaching closes a deal that would otherwise have been lost. Use the calculator above to run your own numbers.

Pricing questions

What currency is the pricing in?
Pricing is in US Dollars (USD). Plans start at US$60/week for up to 3 Overview Users, increasing in tiers based on the number of Team Leaders with dashboard access. Field reps are unlimited at every tier.
What is an Overview User?
An Overview User is a Team Leader or manager who has dashboard visibility over their team for coaching and performance management. Pricing is based on the number of Overview Users — not total headcount. Field reps logging activity through the mobile app are always included.
Is there a free trial?
We don't offer a self-service trial — but a demo gives you a full walkthrough of the platform with your specific use case. Book a 30-minute demo and we'll show you exactly how it would work for your team.
Can we start and stop if our campaign season is seasonal?
Yes. There's no lock-in contract, so you can pause or stop whenever you need to. This makes Outcomes Tracking well-suited to organisations with seasonal campaign structures.
Are there setup fees?
No. Onboarding support is included in your plan. There are no additional setup costs.
What payment methods are accepted?
Contact us to discuss payment options. We work with organisations across Australia, New Zealand, and internationally.
